## What we build

### AI Agents — Sales, Support, Voice & Recruitment

We build custom AI agents tailored to your business function. Whether you need a sales agent that qualifies leads, a support agent that resolves tickets, a voice bot that conducts outbound calls, or a recruitment agent that screens candidates automatically — we design, build, and deploy it.

- Automated outbound calling bots for sales and lead generation
- Recruitment screening automation with auto call interviews
- Property AI automation and outbound calling agents
- Customer support agents with memory and escalation logic

### RAG Chatbots & Document Q&A

We build Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems that let your team — or your customers — ask questions in plain English and get accurate answers from your own data.

- AI Q&A trained on your documents, PDFs, videos, and images
- Internal knowledge bases for teams
- Customer-facing chatbots trained on your product and support content
- Multimodal search across text, tables, and visual content

### Multi-Agent Systems & Automation Pipelines

For complex workflows that go beyond a single agent, we architect multi-agent systems using LangGraph. Each agent has a focused role; an orchestrator coordinates them. The result is a system that handles long, multi-step tasks reliably and at scale.

- Data pipelines with extract, transform, validate, and load agents
- Complex automation workflows across multiple tools and APIs
- Agentic coding pipelines using Claude Code
- Supervisor + worker agent patterns for parallel task execution

### AI SaaS Products & Web Apps

We build full-stack AI-powered SaaS products and internal tools from end to end — architecture, backend, frontend, deployment, and documentation.

- AI-powered dashboards and internal platforms
- SaaS tools with LLM features built into the core product
- Data Q&A interfaces over SQL, NoSQL, and document sources
- CRM integrations with LLMs (Salesforce, HubSpot, and custom)

### Accounting & Platform AI Integrations

We have trained AI on major accounting and business platforms, enabling natural language interaction with financial data.

- Xero, QuickBooks, and FreshBooks integrations with LLMs
- Financial data Q&A and automated reporting
- 3rd-party CRM and ERP integrations with AI reasoning layers
